Effect of Resource Recovery Facilities

MIRA, along with the other resource recovery facilities located in Connecticut, contribute to polluting already at-risk neighborhoods. This follows the trend in the United States, where “80% of waste incinerators are located in environmental justice communities - neighborhoods that are disproportionately burdened by multiple sources of pollution and socio-economic variables” (Budris).
